Al-Madarabah Community - ECHO 2026 Project - December 11, 2025

Executive Summary

ADRA Yemen has completed a comprehensive NEAT+ environmental assessment for the Al-Madarabah community as part of the ECHO 2026 project. This assessment identifies 5 key environmental risks and provides 10 prioritized mitigation measures categorized by implementation priority.

The assessment reveals critical concerns around water quality and scarcity, indoor air pollution, and climate-related risks. Mitigation measures have been prioritized based on cost, technical feasibility, social acceptance, project scope, and implementation capacity.

About NEAT+ (Nexus Environmental Assessment Tool Plus)

NEAT+ was designed to help humanitarian actors integrate sustainable environmental practices into aid. It provides systematic environmental risk assessment with practical, context-specific mitigation measures focusing on water resources, waste management, air quality, soil protection, and ecosystem preservation.
